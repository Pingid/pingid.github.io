citri*
We are a multi-skilled team committed to building enriching digital products.
citri* is built with the belief that what we create is the sum of our knowledge and our experience. Working with our clients is a dialogue of learning and discovery. To this end, our purpose is to transform the insights we gain into products that resonate with people. We find that simplicity and beauty inevitably follow.
